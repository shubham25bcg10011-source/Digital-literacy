# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

Here are 8 actionable steps to protect your digital identity and finances:

### Financial & UPI Safety
1. **Never enter your PIN to receive money:** You only need your UPI PIN to *send* money or check your balance. If an app asks for your PIN to receive funds, it is a scam.
2. **Verify the receiver's identity:** Always double-check the registered name that appears on your UPI app screen before authorizing any payment.

### General Digital Safety
3. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Turn this on for your college email, GitHub, and social media to require a second form of verification beyond just your password.
4. **Do not click suspicious links:** Beware of urgent SMS or emails claiming your "account is blocked" or a "package is delayed." These are often phishing attempts.
5. **Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking:** Never make financial transactions while connected to unsecured public Wi-Fi networks (like at cafes or airports). 
6. **Use unique passwords:** Do not reuse your college portal password for your social media or banking apps. 
7. **Keep software updated:** Regularly update your phone and laptop operating systems to patch security vulnerabilities.

### 🚨 Emergency Reporting Channels
8. **Report cybercrime immediately:** If you are scammed, the "Golden Hour" is critical for freezing funds. Report the incident immediately using official government channels:
   * **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
   * **Helpline:** Dial **1930**
